Why MathGaps should be used in addition to a tutor
More accurate and detailed insights on capability
Tutors are currently unable to hollistically assess the understanding of their students.
Detailed reports on capability allow tutors to plan and structure tutes in a way that suits the student best.
Less tutorial time dedicated to identification of gaps
Tutors spend lots of time identifying old gaps and revisiting past content.
Once gaps are filled, tutors can direct all tutorial time to teaching and learning current topics.
Easily track students progress
Reports progressively update to allow parents and tutors to monitor students progression.
This is useful for evaluating the effectiveness of tutoring.
If you dont currently use a tutor, a MathGaps assessment can effectively determine whether you should.
